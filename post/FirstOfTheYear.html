Hello everyone! This is the first post of this website! Here I want to discuss some things in relation to the website.
First of all, what is its purpose?
I made this website because it serves as a way to share my channel more easily. Afterall, just search LameSniper16 on Google!
It's also a very easy way for me to populate search results with my channel, as well as images. When I make posts, the images used in the post get indexed with Google. Which means I can share my renders more easily too!
In summary, the website is still at its infancy. As of right now, it's just a simple webpage hosted on GitHub, but I do have plans to scale this website much more in the future...
Maybe some functionalities like forums! Either way, it's too early to say. This might be more like 2026 plans. In the end this post is also just a test. Heh.
